In the capital of France, every sightseeing opportunity offers something unique. The Eiffel Tower provides stunning views. The Notre-Dame Cathedral amazes with its Gothic architecture. The Champs-Élysées is perfect for shopping and dining. The Arc de Triomphe stands as a historic monument. The Musée d'Orsay impresses art lovers with its collection. The Luxembourg Gardens offer a peaceful retreat. And, The Louvre is in the capital of France, showcasing thousands of masterpieces, including the Mona Lisa.
